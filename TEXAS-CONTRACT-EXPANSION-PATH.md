# Texas Contract Expansion Path

## Principle
Expand only after the narrow wedge is producing trustworthy signal.

## Phase 1 — Wedge Validation
- Region: DFW
- Category: janitorial / custodial / facility-cleaning related opportunities
- Goal: prove source coverage, normalization, filtering, and user-facing usefulness

## Phase 2 — Category Expansion In DFW
Add adjacent facilities categories such as:
- pressure washing
- building maintenance support
- floor care
- sanitation/disinfection-related service categories
- selected low-complexity facilities services

Goal:
- improve density of useful records
- strengthen the procurement-intelligence product shape

## Phase 3 — Geography Expansion In Texas
Expand to additional metros / regions such as:
- Houston
- Austin
- San Antonio
- major county clusters

Goal:
- test whether the same data engine generalizes across Texas regions

## Phase 4 — Broader Texas Contract Coverage
Add more categories beyond facilities/janitorial once the platform logic is stable.
Possible future categories:
- landscaping
- pest control
- printing
- signage
- selected construction-adjacent service lanes
- eventually more complex categories if the data discipline supports them

## Decision Gates
Do not expand to the next phase until the current phase has:
- stable source list
- credible normalized records
- low enough junk/noise ratio
- clear user value
- repeatable workflow

## Warning
A product that expands too early may become a giant low-trust database.
The goal is not to have the most rows. The goal is to have the most useful rows.
