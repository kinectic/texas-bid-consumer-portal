# Bid Pilot Offer

## Positioning
This is a pilot offer, not a fully mature platform promise.

## Pilot Framing
We monitor a defined set of DFW public procurement sources for janitorial / custodial opportunities and send filtered summaries of relevant items we identify.

## What the Pilot Includes
- Monitoring of a defined DFW source set
- Filtering for janitorial / custodial relevance
- Short opportunity summaries
- Deadline visibility
- Direct links to source documents when available

## What the Pilot Does Not Promise
- Complete market coverage
- Guaranteed capture of every possible bid
- Legal, procurement, or compliance advice
- Full bid-prep or submission management

## Pilot Goal
Demonstrate that the monitoring workflow is useful enough for a janitorial company to save time and discover relevant opportunities they might otherwise miss.

## Pilot Delivery Style
- Small number of curated alerts
- Lightweight summaries
- Honest visibility into source coverage
- Feedback-driven improvement

## Pilot Qualification
Best fit for:
- local janitorial companies
- commercial cleaners wanting public-sector work
- operators without a dedicated bid desk
- companies focused on DFW or nearby territory

## Future Pricing Note
Do not finalize pricing until the monitoring process proves reliable enough across multiple days and a stronger sample pack exists.
