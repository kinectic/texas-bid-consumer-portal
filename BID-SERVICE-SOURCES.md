# Bid Service Sources

## Focus Niche
Janitorial / custodial / facility cleaning for public buildings and schools.

## Priority Source Types
1. City procurement portals
2. County procurement portals
3. K-12 school district bid pages
4. State purchasing portals
5. Community college procurement pages
6. University procurement pages
7. Housing authority procurement pages
8. Airport / transit authority bid pages
9. Municipal facilities maintenance bid pages
10. Cooperative purchasing networks

## Include Keywords
- janitorial
- custodial
- cleaning services
- day porter
- facility cleaning
- building cleaning
- restroom sanitation
- floor care
- carpet cleaning
- disinfection services

## Exclude Keywords
- residential
- house cleaning
- maid service
- medical waste
- HVAC
- landscaping
- security guard
- pest control
- construction
- snow removal

## Initial Geographic Strategy
Start narrow.

Recommended first-pass geography options:
- one state with many public entities and a large cleaning-company base
- or one major metro plus surrounding counties
