# Texas Contract Next Build Steps

## Immediate Product-Build Steps

### 1. Grow the dataset
- keep adding entity records
- keep adding source records
- keep adding awarded and historical evidence
- keep searching for truly open records

### 2. Tighten record quality
- standardize category naming
- standardize confidence rules
- keep status labeling strict
- downgrade uncertain records instead of inflating them

### 3. Prepare render-friendly structure
- ensure records are easy to turn into cards/tables later
- keep fields consistent
- avoid prose-only storage when structured fields exist

### 4. Build the first presentable prototype view
Potential formats:
- markdown mock site
- local HTML page
- simple table-based dashboard

### 5. Only later consider productization layers
- accounts
- pricing
- subscriptions
- alerts
- broader Texas expansion

## Current Product Truth
We are not at polished product yet.
We are at structured proto-product.
That is good progress.

## Rule
Do not chase scale before trust.
Do not chase polish before useful records.
