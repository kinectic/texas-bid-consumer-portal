# DFW Top 15 Monitoring Targets

Ranked for janitorial / custodial public-bid monitoring based on likely bid volume, facilities footprint, portal accessibility, and relevance to a small-commercial-cleaning buyer.

## 1. Dallas County
- URL: https://www.dallascounty.org/departments/purchasing/currentbids.php
- Portal: https://www.bidnetdirect.com/texas/dallas-county
- Why high: county facilities + centralized posting + direct relevance

## 2. Tarrant County
- URL: https://www.tarrantcountytx.gov/en/purchasing.html
