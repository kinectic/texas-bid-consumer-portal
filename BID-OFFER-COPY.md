# Bid Offer Copy

## Core One-Liner
We monitor public bid sources daily and send your janitorial company only matched cleaning contract opportunities in your area, with deadlines, scope highlights, and direct links to the bid documents.

## Short Version
We help janitorial companies find public-sector cleaning bids without wasting hours searching scattered government sites.

## Problem
Small janitorial companies miss good public bids because the opportunities are spread across city, county, school, and state portals. Tracking all of them manually takes time most owners do not have.

## Solution
We monitor the relevant public bid sources, filter for janitorial and custodial work, and deliver only the opportunities that match your area and service profile.

## Value Points
- Less time spent hunting for bids
- Fewer missed opportunities
- Clear deadlines and source links
- Cleaner summaries of what matters
- Better visibility into recurring public-sector work
