## Brief 1 — DFW Airport Custodial / Facilities Monitoring Target
- **Agency:** DFW International Airport Procurement & Materials Management
- **Source:** https://www.dfwairport.com/business/opportunities/procurement/
- **Monitoring Priority:** Very high
- **Observed State:** Procurement page is active and positioned as the main entry point for contract and solicitation activity; current solicitations route through DFW's e-solicitation platform hosted by Bonfire/Euna.
- **Relevant Janitorial Signal:** Airport operations involve large facilities footprints and recurring cleaning/service needs, making this a high-value recurring watch target even when a specific custodial posting is not currently surfaced in lightweight fetch.
- **Current Opportunity Status:** No live janitorial-specific listing captured yet in lightweight fetch.
- **Recommendation:** **Monitor aggressively**. This is a top-tier source for future custodial/facility-cleaning bids.
- **Why It Matters:** Airports are complex, high-footfall public facilities. When custodial bids appear, they are likely meaningful contracts for established janitorial operators.
- **Next Check Focus:** Search for terms like custodial, janitorial, terminal cleaning, day porter, floor care, disinfection, facility services inside the procurement platform.
